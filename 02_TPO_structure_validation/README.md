## Description
This folder contains the structure validation outputs for the homology modeled MPO-domain of the TPO protein (residues 142-738) generated using ProSA-web and Procheck tool of the SAVES server. 

Files:
* *The ProSA-web analyzes the atomic coordinates of the model to detect errors in the modelled protein structures. 
The results are displayed as energy plots and z-scores.*

TPO_energy_plot.png: Illustrates the quality of the model by displaying energy distribution across amino acid sequences. 
TPO_z_score.png: Compares the model's energy profile to that of proteins in PDB. 
                  (z-score- -10.57: confirming the model falls within the expected range of experimentally determined protein structures)

* *The Procheck tool from SAVES server assess the stereochemical quality of protein models.*

Ramchandran_plot.png: The plot provides both the restricted and favoured regions, confirming the stereochemical quality of the model. 
                      The graph shows >90% of residues in favored zones, confirming a high degree of accuracy in its model representation.
